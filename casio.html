The Brand Casio is brought to you in India by Casio India Co. Pvt. Ltd. (CIC), operating in India since 1996, a wholly owned subsidiary of Casio Computer Co. Ltd, Japan; the parent company is engaged in the manufacturing and marketing of various consumer products throughout the world.
Casio India's range of products include sales and marketing of Handheld Calculators, Desktop Calculators, Scientific Calculators, Printing Calculators, Label Printers, Laser-LED based green Projectors, Wrist Watches, Clocks and EMI (Electronic Musical Instruments). The company has also established service centers across the country to provide after-sales-services. Today, Casio is known for its reliable product quality and exemplary innovations and is one of the world's most recognized brands due to its vast product range.

WATCHES G-shock: Designed to resist mechanical stress, shock and vibration. G-Shock is an abbreviation for Gravitational Shock. The watches in the G-Shock line are designed primarily for sports and outdoors-oriented activities; nearly all G-Shocks are chronographs, with either a digital display or a combination of analogue and digital. Other features available in most of the watches watch are countdown timer, world clock, alarms, electroluminescent backlight and water resistance. Newer high-end models in the line also feature GPS, compass or radio-controlled adjustment known as Multi-Band.

Edifice: The Edifice watches are designed to attract buyers engaged in professional careers. The watches have multiple functions, ranging from multiple dials to world time and multiple alarms. EDIFICE, the watch that recognizes the value of speed and time, for people who live in the moment!

Vintage: Vintage watches are based on the traditional styles that have been replaced with the entrance of new styles and designs in the market. The uniqueness and sophistication present in the watches are missing in the watches introduced nowadays. Hence, Casio Vintage Watch takes you back in those days of chivalry and luxury. The opulent look of the timepiece is able to attract the attention of even the most suave men. Whilst being inspired by vintage period, this watch doesn’t lack technological detailing and emphasis the features such as calendar, stopwatch and alarm
